Empowerment

Leap empowers learners to take control of their own learning.

They are given the tools to reflect on past achievements, track current progress and plan for the future.

Convenience

Leap provides staff with speed access to detailed, real-time information in an intuitive and convenient manner.

They are able to track progression for individual learners or at a course-level view.

Availability

Leap enables parents and guardians to participate in the learning process.

A fully configurable permission system means that all information, including timetables, attendance, progress and achievements can be made available, or not, as appropriate in a secure environment.

Low Maintenance

Leap integrates with your information systems providing learner data in real-time with no extra work.

Shibboleth authentication fits with the secure standard already used by educational institutions and Leap can talk to your Moodle VLE.
